# 🏨 Hospitality Performance Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
The Hospitality Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of hotel operations across multiple cities. It focuses on key business metrics such as revenue, bookings, occupancy rate, and cancellation rate to help stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and improve operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall hotel performance across different cities  
- Monitor key metrics like revenue, bookings, occupancy, and cancellations  
- Identify trends in weekly revenue and booking patterns  
- Compare performance across hotel categories and room types  
- Evaluate weekday vs weekend performance  
- Support data-driven decision-making to improve profitability  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Revenue:** 1708.8M  
- **Total Bookings:** 134.59K  
- **Occupancy Rate:** 57.9%  
- **Cancellation Rate:** 24.8%  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- **City-wise filtering** (Bangalore, Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai)  
- **Category filter** (Business, Luxury)  
- KPI cards for quick performance overview  
- Weekly performance trend (Revenue & Bookings)  
- Revenue analysis by hotel and city  
- Revenue breakdown by room category (Elite, Premium, Presidential, Standard)  
- Booking status distribution (Checked-out, Cancelled, No-show)  
- Weekday vs Weekend performance comparison  
- Trend analysis of total revenue over weeks

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue shows a declining trend in later weeks, indicating potential seasonal or demand drop  
- Majority of revenue is generated from **Elite and Premium room categories**  
- **Mumbai and Bangalore contribute significantly** to total revenue  
- Weekday performance is stronger compared to weekends in both revenue and bookings  
- A significant portion of bookings are successfully completed, but cancellation rate (~24.8%) indicates room for improvement  
- Certain hotels outperform others consistently, highlighting high-performing properties  
- Booking trends and revenue patterns suggest opportunities for demand optimization
